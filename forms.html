HTML Forms

A HTML form is an interactive tool used in your webpage that is made up of various controls used to collect/obtain data from your website's visitors/users. The information is then validated, then sent to the server for further processing and/or storage. Exaples of commonly used forms are registration/sign up forms and login/sign in forms
